Abstract

Recent advancements in text-to-image generative diffusion models have made it possible to create strikingly photorealistic images from a single input prompt, showcasing substantial progress in image synthesis. Despite these capabilities, current models continue to face a key limitation: a trade-off between generation speed and image fidelity. For example, single-step generation techniques, such as the approach in LCM, often produce images of noticeably lower quality. To address this issue, we propose UPLIFT-a fast, efficient, and model-agnostic image enhancement framework capable of significantly improving the fidelity of images generated by a wide range of text-to-image models. Extensive experiments conducted across multiple model outputs demonstrate the versatility and effectiveness of our approach, delivering immediate enhancements that achieve high-quality, visually impressive improvements in generated images.
